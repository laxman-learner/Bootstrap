Why CBSE?

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) is a national level board of education in India for public and private schools, controlled and managed by Union Government of India. CBSE syllabus is more student-oriented and helps the students to better prepare themselves for the competitive examinations at the all-India level. CBSE not only prepares students for education in India but helps the student get an edge over others when they apply to reputed institutions abroad or in Nepal.
