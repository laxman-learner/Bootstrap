History

RAI School was established with the sole objective of integrating CBSE education in an excellent learning environment, in the year 2000.

The School is affiliated to C.B.S.E. Board, Preet Vihar, New Delhi, India. Its Affiliation Code No. is 6230006. The School Code No. is 72617. It is situated at Naxal, Kathmandu. In the span of two decades, the School has reached great heights with well appreciated successive outstanding results.

RAI School Today is an Icon of the Nation and one of the renowned institutions in Nepal. The School has achieved many milestones in its history in offering Quality education for the last 20 years. We have 35 Teaching Staff and 30 Non-Teaching Staff. We believe very sincerely that the school will continue to grow and prosper in the coming years.

RAI School proudly announces its success in maintaining its distinctive features by showing tremendous improvement and development in the area of Quality Education & Infrastructure since its inception in 2000. The school has touched the sky with great overall performance and record-setting marks in different subjects. The school has attained the height of success with outstanding results and by crafting the careers of its students.
