School Attendence and Time Keeping

School starts at 8:30 am each day and the students leave school premises by 3:00 pm.

Take child to the main office and sign them in on the computer and inform our administrator. The child can then go to class.

There are times when parents are late in picking up their child from school. If this is going to happen, we ask that the school is informed about the expected delay. Our school is open till 5:00 pm.

And, if a child is still not collected by that time, then the children have to sit in the reception until the parents arrive for pick up.

If you need someone else to come to school to collect your child, please speak to the class teacher in the morning or leave a message in the administration. Each child has a data overview sheet with people who are allowed to collect them.

Call / Inform one day in advance for the absence of the child. The parent has to inform class teacher and our administrator. A message can be left on the reception if the concerned person is not available. If the child is sick and unable to attend school, the information can be given on the day of absence.
