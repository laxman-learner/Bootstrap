Mission/Vision
Mission

Excellence and Learning

RAI School strives for excellence in everything it does, encouraging students to attain both academic success and personal achievement. The mission of RAI School is to provide each student a diverse education in a safe, supportive environment that promotes sound values, self discipline, motivation and excellence in learning. The school assists the students in developing skills to become independent, self-sufficient adults who will succeed and contribute responsibly in a global community.

Vision

We at RAI School believe that each child entrusted to us will receive education in its finest form. Learning is a life-long process and all students and teachers are challenged to exceed their own expectations.

No student will be left behind as they are provided with the skills required for life and work in the 21st century. When students complete their school lives, they will have the qualifications, leadership qualities and sense of destiny to be successful and make a positive contribution to society.
