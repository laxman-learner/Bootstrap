From Chairman's Desk

"A journey of thousand miles begins with a single step."
- Lao Tzu

Rai School has taken the first step towards an exciting new phase. We are geared to go! We are committed to the holistic development of every student entrusted to us. With that in mind, we have introduced state-of-the art facilities, new tie-ups with Indian counterparts and the piece de resistance â€“ an indoor swimming pool.

With a history of twenty years in education, our teams of professional educators are dedicated to helping each student attain his/her maximum potential, both academically and socially. Fostering and celebrating our student's talents and achievements are at the core of all we do every day.

Rai School has provided high quality international education to students in Nepal. Numerous Rai graduates have attended some of the world's leading universities in India, United States, the United Kingdom, and elsewhere.

It remains my humble wish that Rai School continues to shine as a lighthouse of goodness and truthfulness, that in addition to academics, the young ones who pass through its corridors may become caring, productive and responsible citizens and future leaders of the country.

I commend the entire management, staff, student, parents/guardians and all well-wishers for their achievements. I pray that God showers each one of us with His divine grace and blessings, and that our school continues to grow in faith and integrity.

Kind Regards,

Siddharth Kedia
